## Context

Once volumetric distillation works for a single quality level (T-001-03), we need a full LOD chain for organic assets. Different viewing distances warrant different slice counts and texture resolutions.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Generate multi-level LOD chain from the rose distillation:
  - LOD0: 8 slices, full-res textures (close-up / hero plant)
  - LOD1: 4 slices, half-res textures (mid-ground)
  - LOD2: 2 slices, quarter-res textures (background)
  - LOD3: 1 billboard (existing billboard approach, far distance)
- Each LOD level outputs as a separate GLB
- Total budget across all LOD levels < 200KB
- LOD selection metadata (recommended switch distances) included in output
- Viewable and switchable in the optimizer's preview panel
